How to signup?

1. In the Signup screen, enter your full name, email and choose your country. Your international phone number will be selected automatically.

2. Enter your phone number without international code, then click Signup.

3. If you have already created account before, the verification code will be sent automatically to the registered email along with the mobile number you used for registration. Please check that code and enter it in the available field, then click Verify.

4. You account is already set up.
